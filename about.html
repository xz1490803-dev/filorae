Our Story

Handmade with Love

Filorae was born from a passion for crochet and a desire to create beautiful, meaningful gifts. Every piece we make carries a piece of our heart.

What started as a hobby during lockdown has blossomed into a small business driven by creativity and care. We believe that handmade gifts carry a warmth that mass-produced items simply cannot match.


Every keychain, bouquet, and amigurumi that leaves our hands has been crafted stitch by stitch, with attention to every detail. We use premium quality yarn and materials because we believe you deserve nothing less.


At Filorae, we don't just make products — we create memories, one stitch at a time. ♥
