Shipping Policy

1. Processing Time

Since all our items are handmade to order, please allow 5-7 business days for us to create and process your order before shipping.

2. Shipping Methods & Times

We use standard courier services across India. Delivery usually takes 3-5 business days after dispatch, depending on your location.

3. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on your location and will be communicated to you during the order confirmation process on Instagram.

4. Tracking

Once your order is shipped, we will provide you with a tracking number via Instagram DM or WhatsApp.

5. Damaged Items

If your item arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos, and we will do our best to resolve the issue.
