How to Order

Ordering from Filorae is super simple! Just follow these 4 easy steps.

1

Browse & Choose

Browse our collection and find the product you love. Check the details, size, and price.

2

Click "Order on Instagram"

Click the "Order on Instagram" button on the product page. Fill in your name, phone, address, and any special notes.

3

Send the DM

You'll be redirected to Instagram with your order details pre-filled. Just press Send! We'll confirm your order within 24 hours.

4

Pay & Receive

We'll share payment details (UPI/Bank Transfer). Once paid, your handmade gift will be shipped within 5-7 business days. You'll receive tracking info via DM!
